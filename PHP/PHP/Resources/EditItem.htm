Edit Product

Edit products in cases such as a price change, a typo in a product name, or an incorrect stock count.

  1. Find the product in the displayed list and enter the id in the Product ID text field
  2. The details will then display in the text fields down the bottom
  3. Edit the details and press the 'Save' button
