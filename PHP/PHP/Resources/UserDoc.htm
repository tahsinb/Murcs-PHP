Introduction

This application has been built specially for the people's health pharmacy. It is designed to handle instore transactions and to keep an accurate record of all products available in store.

It can also generate reports that provide insight into sales across the past week and the past month.

This documentation explains how to use the software and all the tools available.

For any other questions or queries, please contact Murcs Developement

Login Page

  1. Enter your user id.
    If you're uncertain as to what this is, see your supervising manager. It is important the you log in with YOUR OWN id and not someone elses so that sales can be accurately recorded.
  2. Enter your password.
    You should have set this when you were first employeed. If you are unsure as to what it is or need to change it for another reason, see your supervising manager.
  3. Press the Login button

Details can be removed and the login process restarted by pressing the clear button

Home Page

From the homepage all functions can be accessed through the three drop down menus in the top left

Sales

Make a Sale

Sales can be made by selecting "Add sale" from the "Sales" drop down menu.

This is the primary component of the software through which sales can be made. When items are scanned in, they will appear in the table at the borrom, or items can be manually entered in. If items are to be entered manually, make sure to enter all information

  1. Product id
  2. Product name
  3. Quantity of the product included in the sale
  4. The price of a single unit of the product

There is also a space to enter the customer's name. Always ask the customer for their name but do not force them to give it. The customer's name is helpful for record keeping but not mandatory.

Once the sale is completed select "Save and Proceed" to complete the purchase. Once the sale has been confirmed, the page will load again so that another sale can be processed.

Selecting "Close" will cancel the sale and information will not be saved.

Edit Sale

From here past sales can be edited. This is helpful if sales were made incorrectly or if refunds need to be made.

To select a sale, find it in the displayed list of sales, enter the Sale ID in the available text area and 'Edit'

Once the 'Edit' has been pressed, it will show only the selected sale and text areas will display down the bottom in which you can edit the details of the sale

Once you are happy with the details, select 'Confirm', you will be returned to the Edit sales page and the updated details will be saved.

View Sale

From here sales can be viewed for record keeping. Viewing Sales operates in the same manner as the Edit Sales function but once a sale has been accesed it cannot be modified.

Delete Sale

Take extreme caution before deleting a sale. Once a sale has been deleted, it cannot be retreived. Deleting sales records could lead to potential legal penalties. According to the Australian Taxation Office, sales records must be kept for at least five years.

Only the store manager should delete sales and only for the purpose or declutterring records or removing accidental sales.

To delete a sale enter the sale ID in the provided text field and press the 'Remove' button

Once the sale has been deleted, a confirmation will pop up.

Once a sale has been deleted, it cannot be retreived.

Products

Product and stock management options can be accessed through the 'Stock' dropdown menu

Add New Products

When the store starts selling a new product, it must first be added to the database before it can be included in sales.

  1. Enter the product name
  2. Enter the product price
  3. Enter the number that you have in stock
  4. Press the 'Add Item' button to save the new product

Edit Product

Edit products in cases such as a price change, a typo in a product name, or an incorrect stock count.

  1. Find the product in the displayed list and enter the id in the Product ID text field
  2. The details will then display in the text fields down the bottom
  3. Edit the details and press the 'Save' button

View Product

From here products can be viewed. Viewing Products operates in the same manner as the Edit Product function but once a product has been accesed it cannot be modified.

Delete Product

Products can be deleted when the store no longer stocks them.

To delete an item, enter its ID in the provided textfield and select the 'Remove' button

Once a product has been deleted from the database it cannot be retrieved. If it's removed acccidentally or needs to be added for another reason, it must be added again using the Add Product function

Sales Reports

The program can create sales reports that summarise the sales of the last week or month. Predictions can be made on the expected sales of products based on previous weeks and on the same season in past years.

All of these reports can be acessed by selecting the corresponding item from the 'Reports' drop down menu in the top left.
