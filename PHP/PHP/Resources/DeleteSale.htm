Delete Sale

Take extreme caution before deleting a sale. Once a sale has been deleted, it cannot be retreived. Deleting sales records could lead to potential legal penalties. According to the Australian Taxation Office, sales records must be kept for at least five years.

Only the store manager should delete sales and only for the purpose or declutterring records or removing accidental sales.

To delete a sale enter the sale ID in the provided text field and press the 'Remove' button

Once the sale has been deleted, a confirmation will pop up.

Once a sale has been deleted, it cannot be retreived.
